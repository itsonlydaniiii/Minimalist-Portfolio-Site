My Journey to Programming

Hello! I'm Dani, a passionate and dedicated frontend developer with a background in Tech Recruitment. My journey into coding began when I found myself fascinated by the work of developers I encountered in my previous role. The creativity and problem-solving involved in their work inspired me to dive into coding, initially as a hobby, but it quickly became a true passion.

I'm well-versed in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a growing proficiency in Python. Currently, I'm expanding my skills in React and Angular, driven by a commitment to continuous learning and improvement. Although I'm still at the entry level in my coding career, I bring a strong mindset of perseverance and a willingness to ask for help when needed. I believe that active listening and truly understanding the needs of a project are key to delivering effective and user-friendly web designs.

As I continue to develop my skills, I'm excited about the opportunities ahead and look forward to contributing to projects that challenge me and help me grow as a developer. My goal is to create engaging, accessible, and visually appealing web experiences, and to keep evolving as a life-long learner in the ever-changing world of web development.
